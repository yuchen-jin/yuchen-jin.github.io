about me

Hey, hi, hello, 大家好, I'm Yuchen Jin 金雨晨, she/her pronouns. Born in Shanghai 上海, currently located in NYC.

Graduated from St. John's University with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Asian Studies. I'm a software engineer at The New York Times, building internal tools for data viz. Interested in building actual solutions for real problems and empowering marginalized people through technology.

Sometimes when I'm free, I do cool things like performing, writing and making art and food. But most times when I'm free, I'm probably reading or watching Netflix or playing video games.
